Features

1. The application allows students and teachers to register themselves to access the services of the application. The application also allows students and teachers to authenticate themselves.

2. The application allows students to select a subject of their interest, view the lectures, take tests to check their knowledge and view their scores.

3. The application allows teachers to add new subjects, upload lectures for the subjects, upload tests and score the tests taken by the students.

About Us

Providing the most actionable App

This project is focusing the preparation of an online classroom web application which intends to provides a platform for teachers and students to take their classes online.

This online classroom application allows teachers to upload their lectures online and allows students to select a subject of their interest and view lectures.

Our Mission

The basic objective of this project is to provide a platform for students to register themselves, choose a subject of their interest, watch lectures and take tests to check their knowledge. The objective of this project is also to allow teachers to upload the lectures, upload the tests and score the tests given by the students.
